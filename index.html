Fill in the blank with the correct word that you choose.

The balance of lever is related to mass of the object. When the objects are placed at the same distance from the fulcrum, the lever is going to lean towards the (heavier, lighter) side, and when the mass of the objects are also the same, it will be (balanced/tilted).

Where should Anna sit to make the seesaw move up and down?

A. Sit at the right end of the seesaw.
B. Let Anna hold a teddy bear.
C. Place a teddy bear at the middle point of the seesaw

Fill in the blank with the correct word that you choose.

The balance of lever is related to distance between the object and the fulcrum. When the mass of the objects on the two sides are the same, the lever is going to lean towards the side where the object is (farther/closer) from the fulcrum. It will be (balanced/tilted) if the two objects are also of the same distance to the fulcrum.
